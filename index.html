Headlight Restoration

Replacing headlights is costly and time consuming. Headlight restoration is a cost effective solution that has like new & long lasting results. Yellowing or faded headlights can be corrected effectively. Though a 5 step sanding process and a ceramic coating layer we can guaranteed your headlights will look stunning. We include a 1 year guarantee against future yellowing or fading. This same process can be applied to any plastic light.
